A Home Away From Home

UW Court sits just a few blocks from the University of Washington campus. We rent the entire house as a group rental for UW students, perfect for students who want to live together in comfort. With 10 bedrooms across three floors — many large enough to be shared — and roughly 3,100 sq ft of living space, there is plenty of room for the whole group.

Common areas are fully furnished, so all you need to bring is what you need for your own bedroom.

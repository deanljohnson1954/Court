How to Apply

  1. Call us first — 760-603-0022 or uwcourt@gmail.com to confirm the house is available before applying.
  2. Download the application below and fill it out on your computer.
  3. Every group member must complete a separate application and submit proper identification — a copy of their University of Washington Student ID and their Driver's License or Passport.
  4. Once all group members have completed their applications, submit all applications together as one package to uwcourt@gmail.com.

The application is a fillable PDF — type directly into the fields on your computer, then email or print.

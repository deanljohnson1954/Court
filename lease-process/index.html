Lease Process

Three simple steps to make UW Court your new home

How It Works

We rent UW Court as a group rental for UW students only — the entire house is leased to one group of students. The process is simple and we guide you every step of the way.

Leases run for the full school year, from early September through late August. Tenants pay all utilities. No smoking, no pets.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is UW Court affiliated with the University of Washington?

No. UW Court is privately owned and has no affiliation with the University of Washington.

Who can rent UW Court?

UW students only. Every tenant must provide a UW Student ID with their application, along with a Driver's License and/or Passport.

Can I rent just one bedroom?

No — we rent the entire house to one group only. Contact us for current pricing and availability.

How many bedrooms and bathrooms does UW Court have?

10 bedrooms (4 upstairs, 2 large bedrooms on the main floor, 4 in the basement) and 4 full bathrooms (2 upstairs, 1 on the main floor, 1 in the basement, all with a shower or shower/tub). Many of the bedrooms are large enough to be shared.

What is included in the rent?

Furnished family room and dining areas, two full kitchens (main + basement), washer and dryer in the basement, a gated private backyard deck with outdoor furniture, two off-street parking spots behind the house, monthly professional cleaning of common areas, and year-round landscaping — all at no additional charge.

What about utilities and the garage?

Tenants pay all utilities. The garage is not included in the lease. Tenants may apply for street parking permits through the City of Seattle.

What do I need to bring?

Only items for your own bedroom. Common areas are fully furnished.

Are smoking or pets allowed?

No smoking and no pets.

When does the lease run?

Leases run for the full school year — from early September through late August. Contact us for the current year's exact dates.

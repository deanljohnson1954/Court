About UW Court

5031 19th Ave. NE · Seattle, WA · A few blocks from UW Campus

Prime Location

UW Court is located at 5031 19th Ave. NE, Seattle, WA 98105 — just a few blocks from the University of Washington main campus.  → View on Map

UW Court is a group rental for UW students — roughly 3,100 sq ft of living space spread across three floors, with enough room, bathrooms, and common areas for a full group to live comfortably.

  • 10 bedrooms — 4 upstairs, 2 large bedrooms on the main floor, and 4 in the basement (many large enough to be shared)
  • 4 full bathrooms — 2 upstairs, 1 on the main floor, 1 in the basement (all with shower or shower/tub)
  • Two kitchens — main floor and basement, each with a dining area
  • Large furnished family room on the main floor (couches, tables, lamps)
  • Washer and dryer in the basement
  • Gated, private backyard deck — furnished with outdoor furniture
  • Two off-street parking spots behind the house (fits up to three small cars)
  • Monthly professional cleaning of common areas — at no extra charge
  • Year-round landscaping — at no extra charge
